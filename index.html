ALL THINGS CODE AND ALL THINGS DESIGN IS WHAT I AM ABOUT.

Having graduated with a certificate in graphic design and a NDP in Multimedia Technology I am a very versatile individual. My experience has taught me a lot. Starting out as a UI designer, then moving into front-end developement and eventually into product management, I have been exposed to almost every aspect of building out a digital product. I am excited about innovation, digital technology and I enjoy developing my own process when dealing with products. I am a firm believer of the outside-in method of building products which puts users first and helps solve real problems.
